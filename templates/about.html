About CropCare

CropCare is an Automated Crop Disease Detection System designed to help farmers monitor and maintain healthy crops using AI-powered image recognition. It detects plant diseases in real-time and provides actionable alerts via email and SMS.

  1. Upload an image of your plant or crop through the dashboard.
  2. The AI model analyzes the image and identifies potential diseases.
  3. A detailed report is generated with disease name, severity, and recommended actions.
  4. Real-time notifications are sent via email or SMS for diseased crops.
  5. All predictions are stored securely in the cloud for monitoring crop health over time.

  • AI-based image recognition for fast and accurate disease detection.
  • Real-time alerts through email and SMS.
  • Secure cloud storage for user predictions and history.
  • Comprehensive dashboard to view past predictions and statistics.
  • Supports multiple crops and vegetables with disease recommendations.

  • Early detection of crop diseases to prevent yield loss.
  • Actionable guidance on disease management.
  • Track crop health over time and make informed decisions.
  • Reduces dependency on in-person agronomists.

  • Frontend: HTML, Bootstrap 5
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • AI/ML: TensorFlow/Keras for image recognition
  • Database & Cloud: Supabase for secure data storage
  • Notifications: Email (SMTP) and SMS (Twilio)

CropCare is designed to be lightweight, scalable, and user-friendly, making advanced agricultural technology accessible to farmers everywhere.
